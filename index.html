Background

Founded by Alberto De Arco, Aegis Compliance AI is built on a belief that compliance should be a catalyst, not a constraint. With a background spanning tech, business strategy, and regulatory frameworks, Alberto leads with clarity, vision, and a human-first approach to AI innovation. Aegis is where legal insight meets engineering efficiency. Our mission is simple: to empower a new generation of tech businesses to grow sustainably, ethically, and confidently across markets.

Aegis Compliance AI transforms compliance from a roadblock into a growth enabler.
